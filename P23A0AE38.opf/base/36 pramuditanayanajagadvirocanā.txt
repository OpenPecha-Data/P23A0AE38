prathamo jino vajiranābhi dvitīyu abhūdasaṅgabaladhārī|jinu dharmadhātupratibhāsaḥ sarvadiśapradīpaprabharājaḥ

obhāsasāgaraviyūhaḥ paścimu teṣa praśāntaprabharājaḥ|etān daśa pramukha kṛtvā sarvi ta pūjitā
maya narendrā

smṛtibalena

devamakuṭaśca

asaṅgaḥ

tadanantaraṃ varaviyūhā ratnavicitrasaṃsthitaśarīrāḥ|ratanadhvajāgramatināmā paścima lokadhātu
suvibhaktā

sārocayaśca tada kalpastatra utpanna buddha śatapañca|te sarvi satkṛta svayaṃbhū eta vimokṣa
samabhilaṣatyā

guṇamaṇḍalaḥ prathamu nāmnā śāntanirghoṣa sāgaraśiriśca|ādityateja girirājo lakṣaṇameru megharutaghoṣaḥ

dharmendrarāja guṇarājaḥ puṇyasumeru śāntaprabharājaḥ|etān daśa pramukha kṛtvā sarvi ti pūjitā
maya narendrāḥ

mārgu viśodhitu jinānāṃ yatra samosarī jina aśeṣā|na ca tāva labdha maya kṣāntiryā imamotare
nayu jinānām

tadanantaraṃ surucirābhaḥ śāntanirghoṣahāramatināmā|atra lokadhātu pariśuddho alpakileśasattvaadhivāsaḥ

kalpaḥ sukhābhiratināmā yatra aśīti buddhanayutāsan|te sarvi pūjitā maya narendrā mārgu viśodhito
jinavarāṇām

prathamo jinaḥ kusumarāśiḥ sāgaragarbha saṃbhavagiriśca|devendracūḍa maṇigarbha kāñcanaparvato
ratanarāśiḥ

dharmadhvajo'tha vacanaśrīḥ paścimu teṣu jñānamati buddhaḥ|etān daśa pramukha kṛtvā pūjita te
mayā suranarendrāḥ

arvāgabhūttadanu asti kṣetra sunirmitadhvajapradīpam|kalpa sahasraśirināmā tatra ya buddhakoṭinayutāni

śāntadhvajaḥ śamathaketuḥ śāntapradīpa meghaśirirājaḥ|obhāsayantaprabharājā meghavilambitaḥ
suriyatejā

dharmapradīpaśiri meruarciśiriśca devaśirigarbhaḥ|teṣāṃ ca paścimaku āsīt siṃhavinardito vidupradīpaḥ

etān daśa pramukha kṛtvā pūjita te mayā sugatacandrā|na ca tāva labdha maya kṣāntiryā imamotare
nayasamudram

arvāgatastadanu asti kṣetra samantaābhaśirināmni|kalpo anālayaviyūhastatra ṣaḍaviṃśadbuddhanayutāni

prathamaḥ samantaguṇameghastatsamanantaraṃ gaganacittaḥ|buddhaḥ susaṃbhavaviyūho garjitadharmasāgaranirghoṣaḥ

jinu dharmadhātusvaraghoṣo nirmitameghasusvaraśiriśca|buddhaḥ samantadiśatejā dharmasamudrasaṃbhavarutaśca

guṇasāgaro giripradīpo navamu abhūdathātra jinasūryaḥ|ārāgitaścaramu teṣāṃ ratanaśirīpradīpaguṇaketuḥ

yada niṣkramī sa dupadendro ratanaśiripradīpaguṇaketuḥ|śaśivakradevi ahamāsī niṣkramamāṇu pūjiya
narendram

so me anālayaviyūha praṇidhisamudrasaṃbhavaviyūham|sūtraṃ nigarjasu narendraḥ śrutva mi dhāritaṃ
smṛtibalena

kṣaṇakṣaṇena

jātaṃ hi me karuṇagarbhā maitrinayaṃ samantaprabhamegham|bodhāya cittu nabhatulya buddhabalāpramāṇavipulābham

vitathasaṃjñi

dṛṣṭīgatā gahanacāriṃ tṛṣṇavaśānugaṃ viṣamakarma|gatiṣū anekavidharūpāṃ karmavicitritāṃ samudayantam

sarvagaticyutimukhebhirye upapattibhiḥ samupapannāḥ|jātījarāmaraṇapīḍāṃ kāyikacaitasikāmanubhavanti

pratipannaḥ| yayā cāhaṃ rātridevatayā prabodhitā, sā samantabhadreṇa bodhisattvena
