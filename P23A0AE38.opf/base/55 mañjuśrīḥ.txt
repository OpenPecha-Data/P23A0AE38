55 Mañjuśrīḥ|

atha khalu sudhanaḥ śreṣṭhidārako daśottaraṃ nagaraśatamaṭitvā sumanāmukhadikpratyuddeśaṃ gatvā
atiṣṭhat mañjuśriyaṃ kumārabhūtaṃ cintayan anuvilokayan mañjuśriyaḥ kumārabhūtasya darśanamabhilaṣan
prārthayamānaḥ samavadhānamākāṅkṣamāṇaḥ| atha khalu mañjuśrīḥ kumārabhūto daśottarādyojanaśatātpāṇiṃ
prasārya sumanāmukhanagarasthitasyaiva sudhanasya śreṣṭhidārakasya
mūrdhni pratiṣṭhāpya evamāha-sādhu sādhu kulaputra na śakyaṃ śraddhendriyavirahitaiḥ khinnacittaiḥ
līnacittairanabhyastaprayogaiḥ pratyudāvartyavīryairitvaraguṇasaṃtuṣṭairekakuśalamūlatanmayaiścaryāpraṇidhānābhinirhārākuśalaiḥ
kalyāṇamitrāparigṛṣṭītairbuddhāsamanvāhṛtairiyaṃ
dharmatā jñātum, eṣa nayaḥ eṣa gocaraḥ eṣa vihāro jñātuṃ vā avagāhayituṃ vā avatarituṃ
vā adhimoktuṃ vā kalpayituṃ vā pratyavagantuṃ vā pratilabdhuṃ vā iti||

sa taṃ dharmakathayā saṃdarśayitvā samādāpya samuttejya saṃpraharṣayitvā asaṃkhyeyadharmamukhasamanvāgataṃ
kṛtvā anantajñānamahāvabhāsaprāptaṃ kṛtvā aparyantabodhisattvadhāraṇīpratibhānasamādhyabhijñajñānaveśāviṣṭaṃ
kṛtvā samantabhadracaryāmaṇḍale'vatārayitvā svadeśe ca pratiṣṭhāpya
sudhanasya śreṣṭhidārakasyāntikāt prakrāntaḥ||53||
